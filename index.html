Riskmap.in is an open source initiative by Urban Risk Lab, MIT to map and crowdsource the reporting of urban flooding in Indian cities. This platform will provide real-time actionable information to the emergency responders as well as citizens to tackle the events of urban flooding

Riskmap.in harnesses the power of social media to gather, sort, and display information about the flooding in real time. This platform will provide real-time actionable information to emergency responders, disaster managers to send out targeted alters and coordinate the relief activities and citizens to tackle the monsoon and cyclone-related flooding events.

With the support of TATA Center for Technology and Design at MIT and Greater Chennai Corporation, this project is being piloted in the city of Chennai.
